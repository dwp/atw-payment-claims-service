How to claim

To claim online you’ll need a digital copy of a receipt or invoice, or if you have a paper receipt or invoice you’ll need to take a photo or scan in to upload.

After completing your claim it will be sent to the Access to Work team for review, they will contact you via your chosen contact details if they require more information to support your claim.

Once your claim is reviewed and approved Access to Work will send the payment to the bank details you provided during your claim.
