Guidance

Send us your claim by post

Access to Work can’t process your claim without a receipt or invoice. If you can’t upload a receipt or invoice digitally you’ll have to send us your claim via post.

You’ll need:

  • to complete the paper claim form that was sent to you after your Access to Work grant was awarded
  • to include your receipts or invoices for your special aids and equipment
  • to post your claim to the Access to Work

Post your claim to:

Access to Work Service Centre
Harrow Jobcentre
Mail Handling Site A
Wolverhampton
