Start a claim

To claim online you’ll need a digital copy of a receipt or invoice. If you have a paper receipt or invoice, you’ll need to take a photo or scan it in to upload it.

What happens after you claim

After completing your claim, it will be sent to the Access to Work team for review, they will contact you via your chosen contact details if they require more information to support your claim.

Once your claim is reviewed and approved, Access to Work will send the payment to the bank details you provided during your claim.

If any of your personal information is incorrect or has changed you must make a reasonable adjustments application (opens in a new tab) to update your details. If you have not updated your details, Access to Work may recover any money wrongly paid.
