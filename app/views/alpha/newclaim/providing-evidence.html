Ways you can upload your receipts or invoices

You can:

  • take a photo of a paper receipt with a smart phone or camera
  • take a screenshot of a digital receipt
  • save a digital receipt as a file

Take a photo of your receipt or invoice

Make sure:

  • you are in a well lit place
  • all of the document is in the image
  • you take the photo from above and not at an angle
  • the photo is not blurred
