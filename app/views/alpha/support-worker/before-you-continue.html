Before you continue

What you need to submit a claim (support worker)

You’ll need:

  • to know who bought your support worker (this could be yourself, your employer, or the supplier)
  • the date of when your support worker was bought, (you can find this on the invoice, receipt or order confirmation of your equipment or adaptation)
  • a digital receipt or invoice, or if you have a paper receipt or invoice you’ll need a smart phone, camera, or scanner to upload them
  • the bank details of who bought your support worker

If you can’t digitally upload your receipt or invoice you’ll need to submit your claim with the paper form by post. Your paper form will have been sent to you.
