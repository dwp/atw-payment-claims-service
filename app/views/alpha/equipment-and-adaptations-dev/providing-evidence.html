How to get digital copies of paper receipts or invoices

You can include multiple receipts or invoices when scanning or taking photos of receipts or invoices. This will save you time when it comes to uploading them later.

Your receipts or invoices must show:

  • the cost of the equipment or adaptation
  • the date the equipment or adaptation was bought
  • the full name of the person or company who provided equipment or adaptation
  • a short description of the equipment or adaptation

Taking photos of receipts or invoices

Make sure:

  • you're in a well-lit place
  • you include the whole receipt or invoice in the image
  • you take the photo from above and not at an angle
  • the photo is not blurry

Scanning receipts or invoices

Make sure:

  • you include the whole receipt or invoice in the scan
  • the scan is not blurry
